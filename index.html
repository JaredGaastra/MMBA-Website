Good to Know

Can you guide on the trail?

We currently offer shuttle + digital guidance. A local volunteer sherpa is available if requested to carry extra equipment. You'll receive turn-by-turn GPX routes, on-trail markers where permitted, and live phone support.

Do I need my own bike?

We can arrange premium rental e-MTBs or analog trail bikes sized to you (yes, even for 6'7" riders). Helmets and basic pads included.

What about permits and land access?

We operate only on legal trails/roads and with landowner permission. Private access days are limited—ask about current availability.

How does the shuttle + GPS option work?

We stage you at the drop-in, provide a loaded GPS route (Garmin/Wahoo/Phone), and meet you at designated pickups with water and snacks.
